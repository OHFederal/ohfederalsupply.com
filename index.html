About Us

Built to Serve

OH Federal Supply LLC is a Nevada-based, Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) founded by Samuel R. Howard, a United States Marine Corps veteran with a 100% Permanent & Total disability rating.

We provide commercial products to federal agencies through streamlined procurement channels including SAM.gov, DLA DIBBS, and GSA Schedule. Our focus is getting the right supplies to government buyers quickly, competitively, and without red tape.

As a single-member LLC with low overhead and strong distributor relationships, we deliver value that larger competitors can't match—with the set-aside advantages that agencies need to meet their small business goals.
