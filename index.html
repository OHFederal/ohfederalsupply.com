About Us

Built to Serve

OH Federal Supply LLC is a Nevada-based, veteran-owned small business founded by Samuel R. Howard, a United States Marine Corps veteran with a 100% Permanent & Total disability rating. We are an SBA-certified Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) and Veteran-Owned Small Business (VOSB), certified June 2026.

We provide commercial products to federal agencies through streamlined procurement channels including SAM.gov, DLA DIBBS, and (planned) GSA Schedule. Our focus is getting the right supplies to government buyers quickly, competitively, and without red tape.

As a single-member LLC with low overhead and strong distributor relationships, we deliver value larger competitors can't match — with the reliability agencies expect and the responsiveness only a small business can offer.
